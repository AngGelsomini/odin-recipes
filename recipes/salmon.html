Salmon

Description

Salmon is called an oily fish, mostly due to its high percentage of good saturated fats. It is a full-flavored fish with a very colorful flesh, ranging from orange to dark red and it has a very distinctive flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Brown Sugar
  • Mustard
  • Honey
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Place fillets, skin side down, on a greased baking sheet. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, mustard, honey and salt; spoon over salmon.
  2. Broil 3-4 in. from the heat until fish just begins to flake easily with a fork, 8-12 minutes.
